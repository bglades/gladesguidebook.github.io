About Glades Guidebook

Glades Guidebook is a guide to Colorado hiking, climbing, backpacking, and more. Glades Guidebook will also include guides to international travels. This website is a work in progress and will be updated regularly with new content.

Glades Guidebook was created by Rebecca Glades. Rebecca has a vision to provide
trustworthy information about the outdoors to other explorers.

Created in Boulder, CO by Rebecca Glades
